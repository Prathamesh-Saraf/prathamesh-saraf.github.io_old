Spherical Articulated Manipulator

The project focuses on control of an articulated robotic manipulator.

  • Formulated the Lagrangian Euler dynamics and kinematics for a TRR manipulator.
  • Implemented and evaluated the manipulators performance for the Classical, Optimal, and Impedance controller in multiple operating conditions.
  • Simulated the controllers on the manipulator in a ROS Gazebo environment.
