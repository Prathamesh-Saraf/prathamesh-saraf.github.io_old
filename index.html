About Me

I am a recent graduate from Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) Pilani with a major in Electronics & Instrumentation and a minor in Robotics & Automation. Currently a Research Assistant under Dr. Shishir Kolathaya in Stoch Lab, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, I have been working on the research and development of the infamous walking robot, Stoch. I am actively looking to connect with researchers and industry experts having experience in Motion/Path planning, Robot Control Theory, System integration.

Apart from my work life, I spend my time painting. Please take a look at my art page. Let me know if you like them :-)
