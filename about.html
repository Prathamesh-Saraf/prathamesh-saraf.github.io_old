About Me

I am a passionate researcher aspiring to contribute to the field of Robotics and Control Systems. Recently graduated from Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) Pilani, I have my major in Electronics & Instrumentation and a minor in Robotics & Automation. I work as a Research Assistant under Prof. Shishir Kolathaya in Stoch Lab, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore; I have been working on research and development of the well-known quadruped robot, Stoch. Prior to this, I was fortunate enough to work with Prof. Guillaume Sartoretti at the National University of Singapore on torque-based control of the hexapod robot Daisy by Hebi Robotics.

In my undergraduate years, I had several opportunities to closely work with veterans in robotics and controls like Prof. Alivelu Manga Parimi, Prof. R.N. Ponnalagu, and Prof. Arshad Javed. Throughout the process, I have deepened my understanding of dynamic modeling, system design and integration, optimal control algorithms, and reinforcement learning. My ultimate goal is to make a difference through the work I do. By being an active member of the community, I wish to help it thrive and furbish further.

In my leisure time, you will usually find me painting. Apart from this, I enjoy traveling, trekking. Cooking turns out to be my stressbuster.
